## Case 1: Accounting — categorizing invoices

**The problem**: 2 hours a week sorting and categorizing supplier invoices in the accounting software.

**The solution**: an AI assistant that reads the invoice (PDF or photo), extracts the amount, supplier, category, and pre-fills the software.

**The gain**: 8 hours a month. The accountant focuses on analysis, not data entry.

**The pitfall**: always check the categorizations. AI gets it wrong on ambiguous cases (multi-line invoices, credit notes).
## Case 2: HR — responding to job applications

**The problem**: 50 applications per opening, copy-pasted replies that give a cold image of the company.

**The solution**: an AI template that generates personalized responses in 30 seconds, adapted to the candidate's profile and the position.

**The gain**: time freed up for interviews. And a better employer brand.

**The pitfall**: never send without proofreading. The tone must stay human and consistent with your culture.

## Case 3: Sales — preparing meetings

**The problem**: 30 minutes searching for prospect information before each meeting (website, LinkedIn, news).

**The solution**: an AI prompt that compiles in 2 minutes a prospect brief (activity, recent news, likely challenges, questions to ask).

**The gain**: better-prepared meetings, higher conversion rate.

**The pitfall**: verify the information. AI can hallucinate revenue figures or news.
## Case 4: Customer service — handling recurring requests

**The problem**: 60% of support requests are about the same 10 questions (delivery, returns, warranty).

**The solution**: an AI chatbot trained on your FAQ that instantly answers simple questions and escalates complex cases.

**The gain**: response time divided by 3, higher customer satisfaction.

**The pitfall**: the transparency obligation. The customer must know they're talking to an AI (it's in the AI Act).

## Case 5: Management — summarizing meeting briefs

**The problem**: 15 emails, 3 documents, 2 reports to read before a management meeting.

**The solution**: an AI summary of everything in 1 minute, with key points and expected decisions.

**The gain**: 20 minutes per meeting. Meetings that start with everyone on the same page.

**The pitfall**: the summary is a starting point, not a substitute for reading sensitive documents.
