## The mistake 9 out of 10 SMBs make

When a business owner hears about AI, they immediately think "complex technology", "data scientist", "big budget". And they postpone.

Yet the SMBs benefiting from AI today don't have dedicated tech teams. They simply identified a recurring problem and tested a free tool on it.

The secret is the method: <span className="corail">problem first, tool second</span>.

## 3 concrete examples (no science fiction)

**The accountant spending 2 hours a week categorizing invoices.** An AI assistant can do it in 10 minutes. Gain: <span className="corail">8 hours per month</span>, zero investment.

**The HR manager writing the same candidate rejection emails.** A smart template generates personalized responses in 30 seconds. Gain: time freed up for **interviews that matter**.

**The CEO prepping for meetings by re-reading 15 emails.** An AI summary of the conversation in 1 click. Gain: <span className="corail">20 minutes per meeting</span>, every day.
